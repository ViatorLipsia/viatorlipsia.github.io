Beschreibung


New engine, New gameplay, New adventure, the classic MMORPG 《Grand Fantasia：ORIGIN》technological innovation, bringing an unprecedented gaming experience. Players can explore, fight, and grow in a broader world. Embark on a new adventure journey with your Guardian Sprite.

[Engine Fully Upgraded]

From the land to the forest, Rivers to the ocean, Villages to towns, the game's graphics will be significantly enhanced!
From small details like water ripples and ground plants to large elements like lighting, shadows, and building scenery, everything has been upgraded to be more magnificent and refined, allowing players to once again experience the fantastical and beautiful continent of Saphael.

[Classic Class Gameplay Returns ]

In recent years, Class adjustments have significantly changed the gameplay that players were accustomed to with their original class. In this Orgin, we will restore the previous gameplay basics, allowing players to reclaim their most powerful and proficient playstyles and return to adventuring in Grand Fantasia!

[Optimizations and Remakes]

Compared to previous versions, the Orgin version will feature more optimizations, including batch synthesis, quick split flow, reduced loading times, quick gear switching, and Archive system, allowing players to reminisce about past adventures while enjoying the various convenient operations common in modern games.

[Multilingual Translation]

In this global version, in addition to the existing Chinese language, it will also support "English, Japanese," and other languages, allowing more players worldwide to join our big family~

[Orgin Version Exclusive New Class]

In Grand Fantasia, each Class has its unique gameplay. By combining Talent Cards and equipment, every player can create their own unique style. In the Orgin version, a mysterious new Class will join the World of Grand Fantasia. Let's look forward to it together!
