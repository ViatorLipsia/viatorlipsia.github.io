Beschreibung

Easy Booking
Create matches, segments, and feuds in only a few clicks. With an intuitive UI, running your events is fast, so you can focus on telling your stories.

Wrestling Depth
Delve into a world of wrestling that's rich in detail and options. Manage multiple brands, run drafts, organize tournaments, create factions, assign managers and view deep stats.

Living Universe
Immerse yourself in a dynamic wrestling universe. Compete for awards, rankings and tournaments against your competitors. Experience random events, from snow storms to backstage brawls, that challenge your adaptability and shape your narrative.

Manage Wrestlers
Take charge of your wrestlers' careers. Improve their skills, teach them new traits and determine their creative paths. Book them well and fulfill their requests to keep their morale high. Neglect them, and they could leave - perhaps even for your competitors!

Scenarios
Play 'Passion of Wrestling', a story filled with impactful choices, where you try to keep a company of women's wrestlers afloat in the 1980s. Or if you're new, try the tutorial to learn how the game works. Browse user created scenarios that span across decades, and if you can't find what you're looking for, it's easy to make your own.

Competitors
Engage in ratings wars with your competitors. Slander them and steal their talent, or work out trade deals, all in an effort to rise to the top! Dominate them and you could buy them out, bringing over their wrestlers and championships to your company.

Staff
Manage more than just the wrestlers; oversee your staff including trainers, scouts, producers, doctors and talent managers. Once your wrestlers start getting older, you can transition them into staff members to prolong their careers.

Multiple Game Modes
Choose the way you play. In Sandbox mode, you can let your imagination run wild without any limits. Local multiplayer lets you compete or collaborate with friends. And finally, you can build your wrestling empire from the ground up in Custom Company mode.
