Beschreibung

Too late to fixed
The world is finally in an unreturnable condition. war, chemicals, nuclear, global warming, and widespread plastic pollution causing problems that are too late to fix, and now all humanity pays the price for it.





Third-Person-Shooter
fighting against military bandits and other survivors around the map. keep yourself safe and survive the raid to get all the resources or lose it all with just one bullet from the enemy.





Looting to upgrade
upgrade all the equipment, hideout, and gear to prepare yourself before every match. take out the best of your build and fight every enemy in front of you.





Survive with friend
Host or join a session to play and survive with your friend or maybe… fight with them instead!.





Rare gun from the boss
Get an SVN sniper rifle-type gun that can only drop from ‘Garry’, the big boss in Sarasin area.





Phuket in post-apocalyptic theme
Play in one of the most famous cities in Thailand ‘Phuket’. With an outstanding landmark that can only be seen in Phuket!
